Welcome to Dharamsala


Dharamsala (also spelled Dharamshala) is known as the home of the Dalai Lama, though in fact the Tibetan spiritual leader is based 3km up the hill in McLeod Ganj, and that's where most visitors are heading. Dharamsala proper is a market town a base for all useful travel connections.
